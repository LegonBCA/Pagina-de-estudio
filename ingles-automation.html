Automation

Vocabulary

  • Automation: Automatización
  • Robot: Máquina programable
  • Process: Proceso
  • Efficiency: Eficiencia
  • Productivity: Productividad

Reading Comprehension

Automation involves using technology to perform tasks with minimal human intervention. It increases efficiency and productivity in industries such as manufacturing, logistics, and services. However, automation also raises concerns about job displacement and the need for new skills.

Discussion Questions

  • What are some examples of automation in daily life?
  • How does automation benefit companies?
  • What are the challenges of automation for workers?
  • How can people prepare for an automated future?

Writing Exercise

Write a short essay about the impact of automation on the workplace and society.
