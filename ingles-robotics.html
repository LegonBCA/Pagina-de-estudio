Robotics

Vocabulary

  • Automation: Automatización
  • Artificial Intelligence: Inteligencia Artificial
  • Machine Learning: Aprendizaje Automático
  • Industrial Robot: Robot Industrial
  • Humanoid: Humanoide

Reading Comprehension

Robotics is a rapidly evolving field that combines engineering, computer science, and artificial intelligence. Modern robots are becoming increasingly sophisticated, capable of performing complex tasks in various industries, from manufacturing to healthcare. The integration of AI and machine learning has enabled robots to learn and adapt to new situations.

Key Points:

  • Industrial applications of robotics
  • AI integration in modern robots
  • Future of robotics technology
  • Ethical considerations in robotics

Discussion Questions

  • How are robots changing the manufacturing industry?
  • What ethical considerations arise with advanced robotics?
  • How might robots impact future employment?
  • What role does AI play in modern robotics?

Writing Exercise

Write a short essay (200-250 words) about the impact of robotics on modern industry. Discuss both the benefits and challenges of increased automation.
