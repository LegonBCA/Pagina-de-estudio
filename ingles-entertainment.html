Lectura Bilingüe

Inglés:

To: Employees at Galaxy Advertising
Subject: Gaming/Social Networking Markets

Gaming sites allow advertisers to reach expanding markets for advertising the new line of Centro computers. Their popularity ensures that many people will view the ads. When people talk together online, we want them to see and discuss the new Centro.

A social networking site lets us create a profile and handle related to the new computer. We will update people on the Centro through a social networking site. We could also have a link to a blog with information about the Centro. People will be able to discuss the computer in a forum that we set up.

Gaming sites are another market we should use. Most successful games, like Massively Multiplayer Online Role-Playing Games (MMORPG) feature virtual worlds. These worlds allow us to create billboards that advertise our new computers. We can also create avatars relating to the Centro that people can interact with.

These sites present unique opportunities for marketing. Please take advantage of gaming/social networking markets to sell the new Centro computers.

Español:

Para: Empleados de Galaxy Advertising
Asunto: Mercados de Juegos/Redes Sociales

Los sitios de juegos permiten a los anunciantes llegar a mercados en expansión para publicitar la nueva línea de computadoras Centro. Su popularidad asegura que muchas personas verán los anuncios. Cuando las personas hablan juntas en línea, queremos que vean y discutan sobre el nuevo Centro.

Un sitio de redes sociales nos permite crear un perfil y un identificador relacionado con la nueva computadora. Actualizaremos a las personas sobre el Centro a través de un sitio de redes sociales. También podríamos tener un enlace a un blog con información sobre el Centro. Las personas podrán discutir sobre la computadora en un foro que estableceremos.

Los sitios de juegos son otro mercado que deberíamos utilizar. La mayoría de los juegos exitosos, como los Juegos de Rol Multijugador Masivos en Línea (MMORPG) presentan mundos virtuales. Estos mundos nos permiten crear vallas publicitarias que anuncian nuestras nuevas computadoras. También podemos crear avatares relacionados con el Centro con los que las personas pueden interactuar.

Estos sitios presentan oportunidades únicas para el marketing. Por favor, aprovechen los mercados de juegos/redes sociales para vender las nuevas computadoras Centro.

Reading Comprehension

The entertainment industry has undergone a significant transformation with the rise of social media platforms. Content creators now have direct access to their audience, allowing for more personalized and interactive experiences. This shift has led to new forms of entertainment, such as live streaming and short-form video content.

Social media has also changed how entertainment is marketed and consumed. Viral content can reach millions of viewers in a matter of hours, and engagement metrics have become crucial indicators of success. The line between entertainment and social interaction has become increasingly blurred, creating new opportunities for both creators and consumers.

Discussion Questions

  • How has social media changed the way we consume entertainment?
  • What are the advantages and disadvantages of viral content?
  • How do content creators build and maintain their audience?
  • What role does engagement play in the success of entertainment content?
